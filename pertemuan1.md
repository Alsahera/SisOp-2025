a.	348,654₁₀
    
    Langkah Langkah
    348 ÷ 16 = 21 sisa 12 (C)
    21 ÷ 16 = 1 sisa 5
    1 ÷ 16 = 0 sisa 1
    Jadi, 348₁₀ = 15C

    0,654 × 16 = 10,464 → 10 (A)
    0,464 × 16 = 7,424 → 7
    0,424 × 16 = 6,784 → 6
    Jadi, 0,654₁₀ ≈ A78

    Hasil dari 348,654₁₀ ≈ 15C,A78
